Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

SMA Sniper — Steam Market Assistant ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that automates navigation on Steam Community Market listing pages.

Data collection

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information. No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind is included.

How the Extension works

When you open a Steam Market listing page with the URL parameters page, listingid, and optionally count, the Extension reads those parameters from your browser's address bar and uses Steam's own in-page JavaScript API to navigate to the specified listing and open the buy dialog. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Permissions

The Extension requests access only to https://steamcommunity.com/*. This permission is required solely to inject the navigation script into Steam Market pages. No other websites are accessed.

Third-party services

The Extension does not communicate with any third-party servers or services.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the Extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
